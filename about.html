About FNM Treats

Empowering students, one snack at a time.

FNM Treats is a student-run snack cooperative dedicated to providing quality, affordable snacks on campus. Our team works to source the best treats that cater to everyone’s tastes, from gluten-free options to vegan delights.

Founded by students, for students, we believe in bringing joy and convenience to our campus community. With every purchase, you’re supporting a cooperative that reinvests in student activities, events, and other community initiatives.
